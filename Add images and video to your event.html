Add images and video to your event

You can add an image to your event by going to Build event page. Select Upload photos and video to choose an image from your device. Your event image or video thumbnail will appear in CROWD search results and promotional tools.

In this article

  1. Guidelines for image files
  2. Go to "Build event page."
  3. Add your image.
  4. Crop your image.
  5. Optional: Add a video.
  6. Optional: Add an image to your event description.
  7. Save your changes.

Guidelines for image files

  • File type must be a JPEG or PNG that is no larger than 10 MB.
  • The recommended image size is at least 2160 x 1080px.
  • The width to length ratio for your main image should be 2:1. This means your event image should be a horizontal image that is twice as wide as it is tall. When you upload your image, you'll choose a focus point within the image to be displayed in CROWD search results.
  • You must own the images you upload or have permission from the copyright holder to use them.

1. Go to "Build event page."

Log in to your account and go to Events. Select your event to go to your event dashboard, then select Build event page.

2. Add your image.

Select Upload image to select an image from your device.

NOTE: Design with Canva is temporarily unavailable. If you'd like to upload an image from Canva, create a Canva project with the image size specifications provided above, download the file from Canva, and upload the image to CROWD.

3. Crop your image.

You may be asked to crop your image after uploading it. Your main image must be 2:1, or twice as wide as it is tall.

Once you've selected your image, choose a focus point within the image. This will be displayed to attendees when they're browsing events in CROWD search results. Your focus point will have a 1:1 ratio, which means the width is the same as the height.

4. Optional: Add a video.

Select Upload video and choose your video file. Your video must be an MP4 or MOV file with a:

  • Minimum resolution of 480p
  • Any vertical ratio ‚Äî 9:16 ratio is recommended
  • Duration of 1 minute or less
  • Maximum file size of 1GB

Your video will appear in an image carousel with your event images. If you don't have an event image, your video thumbnail will be displayed in search results.

5. Optional: Add an image to your event description.

If you need to add additional images to your event, add them to the event description.

  1. Scroll to the About this event section.
  2. If you haven't created a description yet, select the plus sign (+).
  3. Type in your description.
  4. Select Add Image when you want to include an image.
  5. Choose your image.

Images you add to your event description won't show in CROWD search or promotional tools, but they will appear on your event listing.

6. Save your changes.

Make sure to save your changes after uploading and configuring your images and videos. Your updates will be reflected on your event page and in search results.
